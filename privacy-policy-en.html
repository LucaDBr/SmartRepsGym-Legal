Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2025

1. Data Controller and Contact

The developer of this app is responsible for data processing. For privacy questions, contact us through the app settings.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, username, profile picture
  • Workout Data: Exercises, weights, repetitions, training times
  • Body Data: Body weight (optional)
  • Device Information: App version, device type for error diagnosis
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymized app usage for improvement

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on your consent (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR) and for contract fulfillment (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR).

4. Purpose of Data Processing

  • Providing app functionality
  • Storing and synchronizing your workout data
  • User authentication and management
  • Improving app performance
  • Technical support and error diagnosis

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted encrypted (TLS 1.3)
  • Cloud Storage: Firebase/Google Cloud with EU servers
  • Local Storage: Temporary data on your device
  • Backups: Regular, encrypted backups
  • Access Control: Strict access restrictions

6. Data Sharing

Your data is not sold or rented to third parties. Transfers occur only to:

  • Google Firebase: For app backend and authentication
  • Apple/Google: For app store services (anonymized)
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymized error data for app improvement

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Information about stored data
  • Rectification: Correction of incorrect data
  • Erasure: Complete removal of your data
  • Portability: Export of your data (CSV format)
  • Objection: Object to data processing
  • Complaint: With data protection authorities

8. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as you use the app. After account deletion, all data is completely deleted within 30 days.

9. Minors

This app is intended for users aged 16 and above. Minors under 16 require consent from their legal guardians.

10. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us through the app settings or support section.
